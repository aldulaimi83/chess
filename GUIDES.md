# 📚 Game Guides & Strategy Tips

Master these games with expert strategies and pro tips!

## ♟️ Chess Strategy Guide

### Beginner Tips
1. **Control the Center** - Place pieces on d4, d5, e4, e5
2. **Develop Pieces Early** - Get knights and bishops out before castling
3. **Castle Early** - Protect your king by castling by move 10
4. **Protect Your Pieces** - Don't lose pieces for free
5. **Look for Tactics** - Watch for forks, pins, and skewers

### Intermediate Strategy
- **Opening Principles**
  - Control center squares
  - Develop pieces before moving the queen
  - Castle early for safety
  - Connect rooks after castling

- **Middle Game**
  - Create threats faster than opponent defends
  - Attack weak squares (especially f7, f2)
  - Keep pressure with piece activity
  - Exchange strong pieces for weak ones

- **Endgame Techniques**
  - King becomes active piece
  - Passed pawns are critical
  - Pawn promotion is the goal
  - Opposition matters in pawn endings

### Advanced Tactics
- Fork: Attack two pieces at once
- Pin: Restrict piece movement
- Skewer: Reverse pin (valuable piece in front)
- Discovered Attack: Move a piece to reveal attack
- Sacrifice: Give material for position/checkmate

### AI Difficulty Levels
- **Easy**: Makes basic mistakes, beginner moves
- **Medium**: Solid play, looks 3-4 moves ahead
- **Hard**: Strong tactics, exploits weaknesses
- **Master**: Optimal play, very challenging

## ⬡ Checkers Strategy Guide

### Basic Rules Reminder
- Move diagonally only
- Capture by jumping opponent pieces
- Reach the back row = King (can move backward)
- Multiple jumps = Must take all available captures

### Winning Strategy
1. **Control the Center**
   - Center squares are valuable real estate
   - Control exit routes for opponent pieces

2. **Build a Wall**
   - Keep pieces together to protect each other
   - Isolated pieces are easy targets

3. **King Creation**
   - Push one piece toward promotion
   - Create distractions while advancing

4. **Avoid Back Row Traps**
   - Back row is your safety zone
   - Avoid the corners early game

5. **Timing Captures**
   - Don't jump into a trap
   - Calculate multi-jump sequences

### Opening Principles
- **Strong Starts**
  - 11-15 (moves 1-3) - Standard good openings
  - Control key squares
  - Develop pieces toward center

### Endgame Tactics
- **King vs Pieces**
  - Kings are powerful - activate them
  - One king can stop multiple pieces
  - Create bottlenecks

- **2v1 King Win**
  - Two kings can force checkmate
  - Cooperate and cut off escape routes

## 💎 Gems Crush Tactics

### Match-3 Fundamentals
1. **Look for Cascades** - Matches create new pieces
2. **Create Special Gems**
   - 4 in a row = Bonus gem
   - 5 in a line = Super bonus
   - L-shapes and T-shapes also special

3. **Use Combos**
   - Chain matches together
   - Special gem + Special gem = Big damage
   - Plan 2-3 moves ahead

### Power-Up Combinations
- **Bomb + Bomb** = Huge explosion
- **Bomb + Star** = Large area clear
- **Color Blast + Star** = All colors match
- **Bombs Near Edge** = More effective

### Level-Specific Strategies
- **Early Levels** (1-10): Focus on basics, understand mechanics
- **Medium Levels** (11-30): Learn power-ups, combo timing
- **Hard Levels** (31+): Plan complex sequences
- **Late Game** (50+): Every move matters, analyze board carefully

### Time Management
- Don't rush - better moves beat fast moves
- Look for cascades before moving
- Plan your combo chain first
- Think 3-5 moves ahead on hard levels

## 🔢 2048 Mastery

### Core Strategy - "The Corner Method"
1. **Lock One Corner**
   - Decide left or right
   - Keep your highest number there
   - Never move away from it

2. **Build Along One Edge**
   - Move all numbers along edge
   - Create line of increasing values
   - Consolidate downward

3. **Maintain Order**
   - Keep high values in corner
   - Smaller values fill remaining space
   - Merge strategically toward corner

### Move Priority
1. **Move in Corner Direction** - Toward your locked corner
2. **Move Sideways** - Fill in the space efficiently
3. **Move Away** - Only if necessary for cascades
4. **Avoid** - Random moves that disrupt pattern

### Decision Tree
```
Can I merge toward corner? → YES: Do it
Can I maintain the pattern? → YES: Do it
Is a cascade possible? → YES: Set it up
Can I create space? → YES: Do it
Else → Accept your fate or reset
```

### Advanced: Avoiding 2-4 Spawns
- New tiles spawn in empty spaces
- 90% are 2s, 10% are 4s
- Plan empty space locations
- Never leave corner area empty

### Level Milestones
- **256** - Easy, just practice
- **512** - Intermediate, need strategy
- **1024** - Good play, consistent corner method
- **2048** - Expert, perfect moves required
- **4096+** - Master player territory

## 🧩 ChromaMaze Expert Guide

### Game Mechanics
1. **Objective**: Navigate to the goal (🏁) while collecting colors in exact sequence
2. **Movement**: Click on adjacent tiles or use arrow keys
3. **Sequence**: Must collect colors 1→2→3→4 in order
4. **Scoring**: Fewer moves = Higher score

### Difficulty Levels Explained
| Difficulty | Colors | Maze Size | Strategy Needed |
|-----------|--------|-----------|-----------------|
| **Easy** | 2 colors | Small | Basic pathfinding |
| **Medium** | 3 colors | Medium | Planning ahead |
| **Hard** | 4 colors | Large | Deep lookahead |

### Solving Strategy

**Step 1: Study the Maze**
- Before moving, examine the entire layout
- Locate all color nodes
- Plan your sequence route

**Step 2: Map the Path**
- Find shortest path between colors
- Note where you can make decisions
- Identify dead ends to avoid

**Step 3: Plan Ahead**
- Look 5+ moves ahead
- Consider which paths allow future moves
- Avoid trapping yourself

**Step 4: Execute Efficiently**
- Move with purpose toward next color
- Don't backtrack unnecessarily
- Use corners to reassess

### Pro Tips
1. **Visual Markers** - Memorize number positions on colors
2. **Grid Sense** - Count steps mentally before moving
3. **Dead End Avoidance** - Don't enter paths with only one exit
4. **Backtracking** - Sometimes needed, plan it in advance
5. **Corner Strategy** - Use corners to change directions smoothly

### Level Progression
- **Levels 1-10**: Learn mechanics, simple mazes
- **Levels 11-25**: Introduce 3-color sequences
- **Levels 26-50**: Complex 4-color mazes, tight paths
- **Levels 51+**: Extreme difficulty, intricate puzzles

### Move Optimization
- Analyze maze for natural "flow paths"
- Look for loops to avoid dead ends
- Chain movement commands efficiently
- Minimize backtracking through planning

### High Score Tips
- **Perfect Play**: Minimum moves = Maximum score
- **Efficiency**: Direct routes, no wasted steps
- **Planning**: Spend time analyzing before moving
- **Patterns**: Recognize maze patterns to solve faster

## 🐍 Snake Classic Strategy

### Game Basics
- Grow by eating food
- Don't hit walls or yourself
- Last snake standing wins

### Beginner Strategy
1. **Follow the Walls** - Move along the edge for safety
2. **Leave Space** - Keep middle open for escaping
3. **Avoid Corners** - Corner traps are deadly
4. **Plan Ahead** - Think 2 moves before moving

### Intermediate Play
- **S-Pattern Movement**
  - Snake fills screen in snake pattern
  - Predictable food patterns become manageable
  - Gives maximum control

- **Spiral Method**
  - Move in expanding spiral
  - Always know where tail is
  - High score potential

- **Quick Turns**
  - Make sharp 90-degree turns
  - React to food locations
  - Stay mobile, don't get trapped

### Expert Techniques
1. **Probability** - Food spawns in patterns
2. **Path Planning** - Map 3-4 moves ahead
3. **Tail Awareness** - Constant body position tracking
4. **Space Management** - Always maintain escape routes
5. **Speed Control** - Don't move too fast early

### Scoring Milestones
- **100 Points**: Casual player
- **500 Points**: Intermediate play
- **1000+ Points**: Expert techniques
- **2000+ Points**: Master strategy
