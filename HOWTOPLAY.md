# 🎮 How to Play - Quick Start Guides

Get started in seconds with these quick guides!

## ♟️ Chess - 30 Second Intro

**Goal**: Checkmate your opponent's king

**Setup**:
1. Click "Play Chess"
2. Choose opponent (AI, Friend, or Online)
3. Click "New Game"

**Quick Rules**:
- **Pawns** (♙) - Move 1 square forward, capture diagonally
- **Rooks** (♖) - Move any distance horizontally/vertically
- **Knights** (♘) - Move in L-shape (2+1 squares)
- **Bishops** (♗) - Move any distance diagonally
- **Queens** (♕) - Move like rooks + bishops combined
- **Kings** (♔) - Move 1 square in any direction

**To Win**: Get opponent's king in "checkmate" (attacked with no escape)

**How to Play**: Click piece, then click destination square. Castling available when conditions met.

## ⬡ Checkers - 30 Second Intro

**Goal**: Capture all opponent pieces or block them

**Setup**:
1. Click "Play Checkers"
2. Choose difficulty or online mode
3. Click "New Game"

**Quick Rules**:
- Move pieces **diagonally** only
- **Capture** by jumping over opponent's piece
- Reach the **back row** to become a **King** 👑
- Kings can move backwards

**Must Jump**: If you can capture, you MUST
**Multiple Jumps**: If available after capture, must take them

**To Win**: Capture all opponent pieces OR block all their moves

## 💎 Gems Crush - 30 Second Intro

**Goal**: Match 3+ gems to score points

**Setup**:
1. Click "Play Gems Crush"
2. Select level
3. Start matching!

**How to Play**:
1. **Click two adjacent gems** to swap them
2. **Form lines of 3+** matching colored gems
3. Gems **disappear** and **gravity pulls new gems down**
4. **Cascades create combos** for bonus points

**Special Gems**:
- **4 in a row** = Bonus gem (wider area match)
- **5 in a line** = Super gem (massive area match)
- **L or T shape** = Special gem

**Power Combos**:
- Bonus + Bonus = BIG explosion
- Special + Special = Massive area clear

**Time Limits**: Some levels have move limits instead of time

## 🔢 2048 - 30 Second Intro

**Goal**: Slide tiles to merge them and reach 2048

**Setup**:
1. Click "Play 2048"
2. Start playing immediately

**How to Play**:
1. **Press arrow keys** to slide all tiles (↑ ↓ ← →)
2. **Tiles with same number merge** when they touch
3. 2+2=4, 4+4=8, 8+8=16, etc.
4. **Reach 2048 to win** (can continue to 4096+)

**Tips**:
- Each move spawns a new tile (usually 2, sometimes 4)
- **Plan 3-4 moves ahead**
- **Keep your highest tile in one corner**
- **Undo available** for learning

**New Tile Rules**: 90% spawn as 2s, 10% as 4s

## 🎨 ChromaMaze - 30 Second Intro

**Goal**: Navigate maze collecting colors in the correct sequence

**Setup**:
1. Click "Play ChromaMaze"
2. Select difficulty (Easy/Medium/Hard)
3. Study the maze

**How to Play**:
1. **See the color sequence** (right panel shows numbers 1-4)
2. **Click adjacent tiles** or **use arrow keys** to move
3. **Collect colors in order** - must get 1 before 2, etc.
4. **Reach the goal** (🏁) after collecting all colors
5. **Fewer moves = Higher score**

**Color System**:
- Each colored dot has a **number** (1, 2, 3, 4)
- Numbers show in **sequence panel**
- **Collect them in order** OR you lose progress
- **Don't panic** - you can restart anytime

**Difficulty Levels**:
- **Easy** - 2 colors, small maze
- **Medium** - 3 colors, medium maze  
- **Hard** - 4 colors, large maze

**Pro Tip**: Study the maze before moving!

## 🐍 Snake - 30 Second Intro

**Goal**: Eat food and grow without hitting walls or yourself

**Setup**:
1. Click "Play Snake"
2. Choose mode/difficulty
3. Start with arrow keys

**How to Play**:
1. **Press arrow keys** to move your snake
2. **Eat the red food** 🍎 to grow longer
3. **Avoid walls** - hitting them = Game Over
4. **Avoid yourself** - hitting your tail = Game Over
5. **Survive as long as possible** for high score

**Difficulty Modes**:
- **Slow** - Easy, great for learning
- **Normal** - Balanced speed
- **Fast** - Challenging gameplay
- **Extreme** - Master level only

**Scoring**: Each food eaten = 10 points

**Power-Ups** (if available):
- Shield - Survive one hit
- Speed Boost - Temporary speed increase

## 🌟 Getting Better

### For All Games:
1. **Start on Easy** - Master the basics first
2. **Play Daily** - Small sessions beat occasional marathons
3. **Study the Guides** - Read GUIDES.md for advanced strategies
4. **Track Your Progress** - Check the leaderboard
5. **Watch Your Replays** - Learn from mistakes

### Key Skills to Develop:
- **Patience** - Don't rush your moves
- **Planning** - Think ahead 3-5 moves
- **Timing** - Know when to act vs wait
- **Pattern Recognition** - See patterns others miss
- **Decision Making** - Quick, good choices beat perfect slow choices
